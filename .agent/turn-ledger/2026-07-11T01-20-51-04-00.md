- Invalid JSON, foreign data, unsupported versions, and completion summaries can all enable Continue.
- The campaign never parses `campaign=new|continue`.
- No selected candidate, decision reason, provenance row, or startup result crosses the route boundary.
- `phantomCommand.save` is written only on victory and contains `{ scene, souls, wave }`.
- Existing checks are source-pattern checks, not candidate-resolution fixtures.
